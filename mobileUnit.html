MOBILE UNIT EXPERIENCE

Make your appointment online

Our new plateform enables women all over Massachussetts to schedule their mammogram online for a faster and more efficient experience on the day of your screening appointment. 1. Visit our dedicated website to look for screening events in MA. 2. Select the time-slots in your area that would be the most convenient INFO : If no events are available in your area, fill in a request, and we would make additional steps to bring an event to your community. 3. Once you pick up a date, fill in your personal information for pre-registration on our mobile unit. 4. A email confirmation and reminder will be sent to your contact information
