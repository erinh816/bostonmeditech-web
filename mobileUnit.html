MOBILE UNIT EXPERIENCE

Make your appointment online

Our new plateform enables women all over Massachussetts to schedule their mammogram online for a faster and more efficient experience on the day of your screening appointment.
1. Visit our dedicated website to look for screening events in MA.
2. Select the time-slots in your area that would be the most convenient INFO : If no events are available in your area, fill in a request, and we would make additional steps to bring an event to your community.
3. Once you pick up a date, fill in your personal information for pre-registration on our mobile unit.
4. A email confirmation and reminder will be sent to your contact information

Your journey at our mobile unit

Our efficient worklow will enable you to take a mammogram screening within 15 minutes. Mandatory COVID-19 security protocol will be performed by our professional team before entering the mobile unit. Those steps will include :
1. Temperature measurement before entering the mobile unit.
2. Survey on health conditions related to COVID-19 for fast tracking of our patients entering our safe environment.
3. Our professional team will check pre-registration information from your side to check if everything is in order.
4. Once the last step is verified, you would be able to get access to our mammography room. The acquisition should take less than 5 minutes.
5. Once the acquisition is completed, and the quality of the mammograms is verified, you will be able to pay for your consultancy in person, or through your insurance.

Results from your appointment
and eventual follow-up service

We vouch that your mammograms will be evaluated by our Artificial Intelligence (AI) as a first set of eyes, before being passed to an experienced radiologists.

The radiologists will provide final diagnositic result based on the AI and his own observations.

Two scenarios might occur :
1. Your mammogram results are benign and no follow-up treatment is required, you would be able to have access to the doctor's report through our website, in order for you to get familiar with your state of health. Based on our results, we will provide an estimate for recurrent mammograms over the years to come.
2. We found some malignant findings and need to provide further diagnosis or treatment. In this particular case, our team will reach out to you through your contact information and guide you in the following steps that needs to taken for further treatment.
